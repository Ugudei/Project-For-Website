Welcome to .Home.

This app uses network address to know what kind of environment you are in and puts the smart-device's sound/vibrator according to your profile that you saved in. For example, when you are in school/work environment(WIFI network) the app puts the device into silent or vibrate mode, once you are out of the environment it puts the device into its default mode. At home it can put your device to loud or the mode you selected it.
